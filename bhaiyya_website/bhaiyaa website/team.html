Our Team Members

Founder and Partner

Meet CA Sumit Biyani

FCA, CMA (Final), LL.B., FAFD (ICAI), Public Finance & Govt. Accounting (ICAI)

CA Sumit Shyamsundar Biyani is a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and a Partner at Biyani Dad & Co., Chartered Accountants. A multidisciplinary finance and legal professional, he holds qualifications including FCA, CMA (Final), LL.B., Certificate Course on Forensic Audit and Fraud Detection (ICAI), and Certificate Course on Public Finance and Government Accounting (ICAI), reflecting his commitment to technical excellence and continuous professional development.

He has extensive experience in audit, assurance, taxation, advisory, and financial consulting, having successfully undertaken assignments for nationalized banks, co-operative banks, government organizations, corporate entities, and business enterprises. His expertise includes statutory audits, concurrent audits, stock audits, system audits, management audits, regulatory compliance, risk assessment, and strategic business consultancy, with particular specialization in the banking and co-operative sectors.

In addition to his professional practice, CA Sumit Biyani is actively associated with commerce education and is regularly invited as a Guest Faculty by educational institutions to deliver expert lectures for Commerce students. Through these sessions, he shares practical insights into accounting, auditing, taxation, finance, and professional career development, helping students connect academic concepts with industry practices.

Known for his analytical approach, ethical values, and solution-oriented mindset, CA Sumit Biyani is committed to delivering practical, value-driven solutions that strengthen governance, ensure regulatory compliance, and contribute to the long-term success of clients across diverse sectors.

Senior Partner

Meet CA Minal Dad

FCA, DISA (ICAI), Concurrent Audit of Banks (ICAI)

CA Minal Purushottam Dad is the Senior Partner of Biyani Dad & Co., Chartered Accountants and a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. She is a DISA (ICAI) qualified Chartered Accountant and has successfully completed the Certificate Course on Concurrent Audit of Banks (ICAI), demonstrating her strong expertise in information systems and the banking audit domain.

With extensive professional experience, she has led and executed a wide range of assignments in the areas of bank audits, internal audits, concurrent audits, stock audits, management audits, audit & assurance, and financial advisory services. Her in-depth understanding of banking operations, internal control systems, regulatory compliance, and risk management enables her to deliver practical, efficient, and high-quality professional solutions to clients across the banking, corporate, and co-operative sectors.

As the Senior Partner, CA Minal Dad plays a pivotal role in the firm's strategic leadership, quality assurance, and client relationship management. Her commitment to professional ethics, technical excellence, and continuous learning has been instrumental in strengthening the firm's reputation for reliability, integrity, and excellence. Under her guidance, the firm consistently delivers client-centric services while maintaining the highest standards of professional practice.

Senior Legal Advisor

Meet Adv. Shyam Biyani

B.A., LL.B. - Senior Advocate

Adv. Shyam Biyani serves as the Senior Legal Advisor to Biyani Dad & Co., Chartered Accountants, bringing more than 28 years of distinguished experience in legal practice and advisory services. A seasoned legal professional, he has been actively practising as a Senior Advocate before the District Court, Nanded, providing comprehensive legal representation and strategic counsel across a wide range of civil, commercial, banking, and regulatory matters.

With extensive expertise in legal advisory, contractual documentation, dispute resolution, recovery proceedings, regulatory compliance, and corporate legal matters, he provides valuable guidance to individuals, businesses, financial institutions, and co-operative organizations. His practical approach and in-depth understanding of the legal framework enable clients to address complex legal challenges with confidence while ensuring compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

As the firm's Senior Legal Advisor, Adv. Shyam Biyani strengthens Biyani Dad & Co.'s multidisciplinary approach by integrating legal expertise with financial and business advisory services. His vast courtroom experience, professional integrity, and client-focused approach enable the firm to deliver comprehensive and effective solutions that address both legal and commercial aspects of clients' requirements.
