About us

Biyani Dad & Co. started in 2018 is a Chartered Accountant firm based out of Nanded, we work with Adv. Shyam Biyani, who have been around since 1990 in field of advocacy and together we offer services in the field of accounts, statutory audits, taxation and Other legal matters. We offer a range of services to meet the complex and evolving accounting, tax and compliance challenges that you face on multiple fronts. Mission of our firm is to provide clients with the 'one stop Solution' for all their businesses, financial and regulatory requirement.
