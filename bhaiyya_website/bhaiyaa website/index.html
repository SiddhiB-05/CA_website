About us

Biyani Dad & Co., Chartered Accountants is a professionally managed partnership firm established in 2018, headquartered in Nanded with a branch office in Nizamabad. Registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, we deliver professional services built on integrity, technical expertise, and a client-first approach.

We offer audit and assurance, taxation, accounting, financial advisory, management consultancy, regulatory compliance, and business support services. With experience across banking, government, corporate, and commercial sectors, we provide practical and timely solutions that strengthen governance, ensure compliance, improve reporting, and support informed business decisions.

Backed by a Senior Legal Advisor with more than 28 years of experience, we also provide integrated legal and regulatory guidance for contractual, compliance, governance, and business-related matters.

Over the years, Biyani Dad & Co. has served banks, government departments, corporates, partnership firms, trusts, societies, start-ups, MSMEs, and other business organizations. Guided by integrity, professionalism, quality, and excellence, we focus on long-term relationships and reliable services that create sustainable value.
